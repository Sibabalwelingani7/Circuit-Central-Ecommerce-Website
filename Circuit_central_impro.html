Project Plan

Main Pages & Features

1. Shop Page (Product Listing)

Product discovery with filters, search and clear product data.

  • Display product images, name, short & long descriptions, price and stock status.
  • Categories & filters: brand, price range, type (e.g., laptops, desktops, accessories).
  • Search bar for quick lookups and keyword matching.

2. Promos Page

Highlight current offers.

  • List active discounts, coupons and bundle deals.
  • Automatic expiry handling and promo codes at checkout.

3. My Account (User Accounts)

User management & order history.

  • Signup/login (email, social optional), profile, saved addresses and payment methods.
  • Order history, order tracking, saved items / wishlists.

4. Shopping Cart

Review & adjust before buying.

  • Add/remove items, adjust quantities and view subtotal, tax and shipping estimates.
  • Persist cart for logged-in users across devices.

5. Trade-in Page

Device trade-ins for credit.

  • Form for customers to submit device details and get an estimated trade-in value.
  • Option to accept store credit or discount on purchase.

6. Checkout System

Complete purchases securely.

  • Collect shipping details, choose shipping method, apply coupons and select payment.
  • Order confirmation page and email receipt.

7. Payment Integration

Support multiple secure payment methods.

  • Credit/Debit cards, PayPal, Bank Transfer, Mobile Money (where applicable).
  • PCI-compliant payment flow or use trusted gateway (Stripe, PayGate, etc.).

8. Order Management (Admin)

Admin tools for orders & fulfillment.

  • View & process orders, update fulfillment status, manage returns and refunds.
  • Inventory adjustments after sales or trade-ins.

Extra Ideas

  • Newsletter & marketing emails for promos.
  • Customer support center / FAQ and chat integration.
  • Product recommendations, analytics dashboard and sales reports.
